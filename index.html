Trinity disAbility Service Journey 2000 - 2025

Welcome to the 25th Anniversary of Trinity College Dublin's DisAbility Service, marking a quarter-century of dedication to fostering an inclusive and supportive environment for students with disabilities. Since its establishment in 2000, the service has played a pivotal role in transforming Trinity into a leader in accessibility, ensuring every student can fully participate in college life and achieve their academic and personal goals.

This online version of “25 Years of the Trinity disAbility Service” celebrates the journey of the Service from its formal establishment in 2000, when a full-time Disability Officer was appointed and the disAbility Service (DS) was set up with the employment of a Disability Service Coordinator (the present Director, Declan Treanor). It showcases key milestones, innovations, and student-led initiatives that have shaped a more accessible and inclusive Trinity College Dublin, and introduces the next chapter of our work through the disAbility Action Plan 2025-2030.
